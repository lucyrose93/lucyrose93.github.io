Private Yoga


Are you seeking focussed guidance for your yoga practice? In a 1-to-1 or small group situation, yoga instruction is entirely tailored to you, and how you are on the day. The aim is to build confidence and motivation so that you can establish a self-practice. Some choose to sign up long term, others for a shorter period while tending to an injury, for example. For sure, you will shift towards greater self-awareness and self-assurance on and off the yoga mat. When you go to a movement class – or when you face a life event – you will know have an amazing tool belt for looking after your body and energy levels.

My private classes are characterised by constant assists with your alignment, which are informed by my training in Thai Yoga Massage. Each new student will be interviewed about their personal history and motivation for seeking private yoga.

Please note that cancellations within 24-hours of the agreed time will cost 50% of the agreed class fee. Do get in touch if you have questions. I am happy to speak on the phone.

Workspace Yoga


Yoga can be part of a healthy work-life balance, helping to make your office environment happier and more productive. Here are a few benefits of corporate yoga:


  • Reduced back pain (Hartfiel et al. in Occupational Medicine, 2012)
  • Lower burnout rates (Alexander et al. in Sage, 2015)
  • Less hostility and aggression (Dwivedi et al. in Ayu, 2015)
  • Less absenteeism
  • Reduced stress
  • Greater concentration
