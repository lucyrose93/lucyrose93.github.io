About Me

Hi there, I'm Lucy.
I grew up practising yoga with my mother, a yoga teacher trainer.
She taught me the importance of making yoga accessible to everyone.
I am particularly passionate about aligning yogic techniques with achieving other goals in life, be they mental or physical.
My classes are a mix of Hatha (traditional) yoga blended with the creativity of Vinyasa Flow (breath co-ordinated with movement).
In addition to teaching, I’m a member of the Leadership Council for Embodied Yoga Principles.
My writings on the mind-body connection have been published in Om Magazine and Yoga International, where I hold a rating of 9.1/10 from over 750 reviewers.
I am a certified and insured member of Yoga Alliance Professionals.
