About Me

Hi there! I'm Lucy, a certified and insured Yoga Alliance professional, and member of the Leadership Council for Embodied Yoga Principles.
I grew up practising yoga with my mother, a yoga teacher trainer.
She taught me the importance of making asana accessible to everyone, with modifications for different body types and easy-to-follow guidance.
My classes are a mix of Hatha (traditional) yoga blended with the creativity of Vinyasa Flow (breath co-ordinated with movement).
I truly believe that the power of the breath and conscious movement cannot be underestimated. My proof? I swam to the Isle of Wight in 2016, and it actually felt effortless, thanks to yogic breathing.
It just goes to show that yoga can be aligned with achieving other goals in life, be they physical or mental. Yoga is empowering because it teaches that you already have the key to success and happiness inside of you; you are enough as you are.
"Yoga Karmasu Kaushalam!" ("Excellence in action is yoga!")
If you're interested in finding out more, some of my writings on the mind-body connection have been published in Om Magazine and Yoga International, where I hold a rating of 9.1/10 from over 800 reviewers.
