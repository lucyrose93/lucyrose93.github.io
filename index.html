About Me, Lucy.

I'm a certified and insured member of Yoga Alliance Professionals, offering private and group classes in and around East London.
I'm also a member of the Leadership Council for Embodied Yoga Principles and a fully qualified EYP teacher, specialising in self-development and partner yoga.
I grew up practising yoga with my mother, a yoga teacher trainer.
She taught me the importance of making postures accessible to everyone, with modifications for different body types and easy-to-follow guidance.
My classes are a mix of Hatha (traditional) yoga blended with the creativity of Vinyasa Flow (breath co-ordinated with movement).
I truly believe that while yoga provides a great physical workout, it can also be aligned with achieving other goals in life, helping you to be calmer and composed off the mat.
Yoga is empowering because it reconnects you with your higher potential.
If you're interested in finding out more, some of my writings on the mind-body connection have been published in Om Magazine and Yoga International, where I hold a rating of 9/10 from over 1,000 reviewers.
