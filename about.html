About Lucy

Lucy is a passionate teacher and learner with a personal practice that spans 9 years. Growing up, she spent a lot of time at her mother's yoga studio. After studying French, Spanish, and Philosophy at Durham University, Lucy taught herself to code and became a freelance website designer and developer. It was an outlet for her creative and analytical mind, yet she felt a deep need to share her yoga. She considered training as a teacher under the tutelage of her mother, but decided to carve her own path and headed to India for her accreditation. There, she realised that teaching yoga will be a humbling, lifelong commitment.

In addition to her 200hr qualification in Hatha Yoga, Lucy has trained in Embodied Yoga Principles (45hrs), Thai Yoga Massage (48hrs), and Vajrasati Yoga, which draws on alignment principles from Iyengar and ancient philosophies (500hrs en route). From September 2018, Lucy is embarking on a Masters in Research at the Royal College of Art. As an academic, she is keen to apply yogic principles to the field of Communication Design.

Lucy is commited to making her yoga classes accessible, consensual, body positive, educational, creative, experiential, humble, loving, balanced, and empowering.
