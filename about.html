About Me, Lucy.

I teach Hatha (classical) yoga and Vinyasa Flow (breath co-ordinated with flowing sequences) to a lovely, diverse family of students.
I have also been known to lead workshops in Partner Yoga and self-development, using my training in Embodied Yoga Principles.
I continue to learn and incorporate new skills into my teaching vocabulary, including Thai Yoga Massage and Body Positivity.
Some of my writings on the mind-body connection have been published in Om Magazine, Yoga International (9/10 from over 1,300 reviewers), and Sivana East.
Alongside yoga, from September 2018 I will be studying for an Masters in Research that applies yogic principles to communication design, at the Royal College of Art.
