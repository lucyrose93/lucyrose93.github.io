About Me, Lucy.

I'm a certified and insured member of Yoga Alliance Professionals, offering private and group classes in and around East London.
My classes are a mix of Hatha (traditional) yoga blended with the creativity of Vinyasa Flow (breath co-ordinated with movement).
I also teach self development workshops following Embodied Yoga Principles.
Some of my writings on the mind-body connection have been published in Om Magazine and Yoga International, where I hold a rating of 9/10 from over 1,300 reviewers.
Feel free to drop me a line. Let's connect!
