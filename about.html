About Me, Lucy.

My journey with yoga started from a very young age; my mother is a Yoga Teacher Trainer and I used to hang out a lot at her studio!
Nowadays, I teach Hatha (classical) yoga and Vinyasa Flow (breath co-ordinated with flowing sequences) to my own lovely family of students.
I have also been known to lead Partner Yoga workshops that incorporate acrobatic elements, as well as self-development workshops that follow Embodied Yoga Principles.
I continue to learn and incorporate new skills into my teaching vocabulary, including Thai Yoga Massage and coaching for Body Positivity.
Some of my writings on the mind-body connection have been published in Om Magazine and Yoga International (9/10 from over 1,300 reviewers).
Feel free to drop me a line. Let's connect!
