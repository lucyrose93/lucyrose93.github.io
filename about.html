About Me, Lucy.

My first training was in Hatha yoga at a traditional school in India. Vinyasa Flow is the main style I teach nowadays. It is popular for honing the mind, body and breath in a beautifully flowing sequence.

My teaching vocabulary is influenced by Embodied Yoga Principles (EYP), Thai Yoga Massage and Body Positivity. Some of my writings on the mind-body connection have been published in Om Magazine, Yoga International (9/10 from over 1,300 reviewers), and Sivana East. I also have a free e-book, published with EYP.

Alongside yoga, from September 2018 I will be studying for a Masters in Research that applies yogic principles to Communication Design, at the Royal College of Art. For my undergraduate degree, I read Philosophy, French and Spanish at Durham University, obtaining a First Class while captaining a women's football team and working as a freelance travel writer.

But that's enough about me. How could I help you?
